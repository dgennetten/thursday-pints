### Tabs
 **Breweries**: View all breweries sorted by last visit date
 **Ranked**: See breweries sorted by popularity (most visits). Pins are colored from red (most visits) to gray (least visits)
 **Tour**: View all visits in chronological order

### Map View
Toggle the **Map** button to show or hide the interactive map. When active, the map displays side-by-side with the list for easy navigation.

### Filtering
Use the **Filter** box at the top of each list to search by brewery name, date, or notes. The list and map will automatically update to show only the filtered breweries.

### Selecting Items
Click on any brewery in the list to zoom the map directly to that location. 
Click on any pin on the map to see a popup with brewery details. The address in the popup is clickable and will open your default map app for easy directions.
